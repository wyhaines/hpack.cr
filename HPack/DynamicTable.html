    • HPack::DynamicTable
    • Deque({String, String})
    • Reference

    Overview

    The DynamicTable is a table of header names and values. It is implemented as a subclass of a Deque in order to get access to all of the iteration and interrogation methods without having to write methods which explicitly wrap them. Fewer lines to maintain is a win. As a caveat, though, do not interact with the storage or deletion of data via any methods other than #add and #clear, as the native Deque methods will not keep an accurate tally of the bytesize of the structure.
